```

```

### Awaiting Difficulty Selection
```
AI Review section displays with full content
```

```
[Spinner] Submitting...
```

- Background: Blue-600
- Hover: Blue-700
- Disabled: Gray-400

### Secondary Buttons

- Border: 1px Gray-200
- Hover: Gray-100
- Width: Full (w-full)
